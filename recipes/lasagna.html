Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat the layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for 30 minutes.
