Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken Curry is a flavorful dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a spicy and aromatic gravy. It's a perfect accompaniment to rice or flatbreads, offering a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (500g) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the turmeric, chili powder, cumin powder, and coriander powder. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well with the spices. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add water, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook on medium heat until tender (about 20 minutes).
  7. Pour in the coconut milk, mix well, and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Adjust salt to taste and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  9. Serve hot with rice or naan.
