Fresh Garden Salad

Description

Fresh Garden Salad is a simple and healthy dish made with a mix of fresh vegetables and a light, zesty dressing. It's a refreshing side dish that pairs well with any meal or can be enjoyed on its own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and shredded carrots.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately as a side dish or light meal.
